My Roller Coaster Ride

Born in Lawrence, Massachusetts, raised in Puerto Rico. I first entered college thinking I was going to be an electrical engineer. Three years later I decided to change concentration to become an event producer. I did a few events for a couple of years and then went into real estate. Once I passed the test to become a real estate agent, the agency told me that my license would not come in for at least 6-8 months. During this down time I decided to self learn coding on a whim. Needless to say I never sold a single estate. Now I’m finishing my Master’s in Software Development at Boston University.

My Love for Games

I found my love for video games early with Super Mario Bros. After that I played anything I could put my hands on until I discovered Marvel vs Capcom 2 in high school. In my first few years of college the game’s sequel ( Marvel vs Capcom 3 ) came out. I fell so in love with the game that I started playing it competitively going to tournaments trying to be the best. Naturally, as I get my degree in Software Development, I see myself working for a video game company.
